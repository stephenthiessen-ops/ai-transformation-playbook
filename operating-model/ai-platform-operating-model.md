# AI Platform Operating Model

## Purpose

Define how AI capabilities are provisioned, governed, and scaled across engineering and business functions.

---

## Core Components

### 1. Tooling Layer
- Approved LLM providers
- Secure API gateways
- Version-controlled prompt libraries

### 2. Data Layer
- Structured, governed datasets
- Defined data contracts
- Access boundaries

### 3. Intelligence Layer
- Deterministic scoring models
- AI interpretation agents
- Executive narrative systems

### 4. Governance Layer
- Model usage review
- Risk scoring
- Human-in-the-loop approval

### 5. Measurement Layer
- Adoption metrics
- Productivity deltas
- Decision velocity tracking

---

## Organizational Structure

- AI Platform Lead
- Governance / Risk Partner
- Enablement & Training Lead
- Embedded AI Champions
